Negros Occidental Justice Resource Center

Our mission is to provide information, resources, and support to individuals involved in the criminal justice system. This include scheduling for legal assistance, government contact centers and basic human rights information. Our goal is to help individuals navigate the criminal justice system and improve their chances of successfully re-entering society. We aim to promote the justice system reform.

The PUBLIC ATTORNEY'S OFFICE - (PAO) is an attached agency of the Department of Justice which provides indigent litigants free legal assistance. MANDATE. Provide indigent sector access to counsel at the time of need.

Objectives

Negros Occidental Justice Resource Center objectives include:

  • Providing legal assistance and representation to underserved or marginalized populations.
  • Educating the public about their legal rights and responsibilities and the court system.
  • Collaborating with other organizations and agencies to provide comprehensive services and resources to individuals.
  • Advocating for the rights of the marginalized and underrepresented groups in the court system and promoting fair and equal access to justice for all.
  • The overall goal of Negros Occidental Justice Resource Center is to serve as a resource to empower individuals and communities to navigate the justice system and promote fairness and equity in the legal system.
